# 8th Grade Curriculum — Self-Built (Free Resources)

WA target: 1,000 hours / 180 days ≈ 28 hrs/week (36-week year, 5 days/week).

| Subject | Primary Resource | Link | Weekly Hrs |
|---|---|---|---|

| Reading | Library books + CommonLit | https://www.commonlit.org | 4 |

| Science | CK-12 — Physical/Life Science FlexBooks | https://www.ck12.org | 4 |

**Total: ~28 hrs/week**
